knotless braids history

Knotless braids have been around since the early 2000's but after years of being on the fringe of hair braiding they've taken over. The term knotless references the knot used to start traditional box braids. With traditional box braids, the braider would secure the braiding hair tightly to the scalp, forming a knot.
